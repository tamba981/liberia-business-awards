Liberian Gold Coffee: Brewing International Success

Export Excellence Award 2024

From Local to Global

Liberian Gold Coffee began as a small cooperative of coffee farmers in Nimba County, struggling to find markets beyond local borders. Under the leadership of Michael B. Kromah, the company transformed traditional coffee farming into a premium export business.

"Liberia has some of the world's best coffee-growing conditions, but our beans were being sold as commodities without proper recognition of their quality," explains Kromah. "We knew we had to change this narrative."

The Export Excellence Award Impact

Winning the Export Excellence Award in 2024 provided the international credibility Liberian Gold Coffee needed to break into premium markets.

"The award was our passport to international recognition," says Kromah. "European and American buyers who had been hesitant to source from Liberia suddenly saw us as a legitimate, quality-focused supplier. We went from struggling to get meetings to being approached by international distributors."

"The Export Excellence Award validated our quality standards internationally. We now ship to Germany, France, UK, and the US. LBA put Liberian coffee on the global map."

- Michael B. Kromah, Export Manager

Quality and Sustainability

Key to their success has been an unwavering commitment to quality and sustainable practices:

  • Implementation of international quality certification standards
  • Development of direct trade relationships with international roasters
  • Investment in modern processing facilities
  • Fair trade practices ensuring farmers receive premium prices
  • Organic farming methods preserving Liberia's biodiversity

Economic Impact

Liberian Gold Coffee's success has created significant economic benefits:

  • Increased income for 200+ coffee farming families
  • Created 50 permanent jobs in processing and logistics
  • Generated $2 million in annual export revenue
  • Attracted international coffee buyers to Liberia
  • Inspired other agricultural exporters to pursue quality over quantity

Global Ambitions

Looking forward, Liberian Gold Coffee aims to:

  • Establish Liberia as a premium coffee origin globally
  • Expand to 10+ international markets
  • Develop value-added products like roasted coffee and capsules
  • Create a coffee tourism experience in Liberia
  • Double farmer participation by 2027
