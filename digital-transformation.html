AgroTech Liberia

Mobile Market Platform

Developed an app connecting 5,000+ farmers directly with buyers, reducing post-harvest losses by 60% and increasing farmer incomes by 45%.

"Our app eliminated 4 middlemen. Farmers now get fair prices, buyers get fresh produce, everyone wins."

Liberia Logistics Pro

Smart Logistics Platform

AI-powered delivery optimization reduced delivery times by 40% and fuel costs by 25%. Serves 200+ businesses across 8 counties.

"Technology turned our biggest challenge—poor infrastructure—into our greatest opportunity."

FinAccess Liberia

Digital Banking Solutions

Mobile banking platform serving 50,000+ previously unbanked Liberians. Processed $15M in transactions in first year.

"Financial inclusion starts with digital inclusion. We're bringing banking to every smartphone."
