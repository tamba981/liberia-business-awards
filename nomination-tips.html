5 Expert Tips to Strengthen Your Award Nomination

March 15, 2026
10 min read
Nomination Tips, Award Strategy, Business Recognition

Submitting a winning nomination requires more than just filling out a form. Based on insights from past winners and judges, here are 5 expert tips to make your Liberia Business Awards nomination stand out from the competition.

1

Tell a Compelling Story

Why it matters: Judges remember stories, not statistics.

Pro Tip: Start with your business's origin story. What problem did you solve? What challenges did you overcome?

  • Humanize your business journey
  • Highlight pivotal moments
  • Connect emotionally with judges
2

Quantify Your Impact

Why it matters: Numbers provide credibility and scale.

Pro Tip: Use specific metrics to demonstrate growth, impact, and success.

  • Revenue growth percentages
  • Job creation numbers
  • Customer satisfaction rates
  • Community impact metrics
3

Align with Category Criteria

Why it matters: Each category has specific judging criteria.

Pro Tip: Tailor your submission to directly address each point in the category description.

  • Read category descriptions carefully
  • Address all criteria explicitly
  • Provide category-specific evidence
4

Include Strong Testimonials

Why it matters: Third-party validation adds credibility.

Pro Tip: Collect testimonials from customers, partners, and employees that speak to specific achievements.

  • Include customer quotes
  • Add partner endorsements
  • Feature employee testimonials
5

Showcase Innovation

Why it matters: Innovation demonstrates forward-thinking leadership.

Pro Tip: Highlight unique approaches, technologies, or business models that set you apart.

  • Describe unique processes
  • Highlight technological innovation
  • Showcase creative solutions

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Avoid These Pitfalls:

  • Generic, copy-pasted content
  • Missing supporting evidence
  • Submitting incomplete information
  • Ignoring word limits
  • Waiting until the last minute
