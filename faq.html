Nomination Process

What are the precise nomination dates for the annual cycle?

Annual Timeline:

  • Nominations Open: March 1 each year
  • Nominations Close: May 30 at 5:59 PM Liberia Time
  • No extensions granted - Strict deadline enforcement
  • Early submission encouraged for thorough review

Pro Tip: Submit by May 15 to allow time for any required documentation corrections.

What documentation is required for nomination?

Mandatory Documentation:

  • Business Registration Certificate
  • Valid Business License
  • Tax Clearance Certificate (last 2 years)
  • Audited Financial Statements (or management accounts)
  • Organizational Chart
  • Proof of Operations (photos, premises documentation)

Supporting Documentation (Recommended):

  • Client testimonials/references
  • Media coverage/press clippings
  • Certifications/awards received
  • CSR/Sustainability reports
  • Innovation/patent documentation
Can international businesses with Liberian operations apply?

International Business Eligibility:

  • Must have physical operations in Liberia
  • Minimum 50% of leadership team based in Liberia
  • Significant economic contribution to Liberia
  • Compliance with all Liberian regulations
  • Preference given to businesses with Liberian ownership/partnership

Note: We encourage joint ventures and partnerships that build local capacity.

Evaluation & Voting Process

How does the voting process work (June-July 30)?

Two-Tier Voting System:

1. Expert Jury Panel (70% weight):

  • Industry experts (local & international)
  • Previous award winners
  • Academic professionals
  • Evaluation based on 10-point criteria matrix
  • Blind evaluation process

2. Public Voting (30% weight):

  • Open from June 1 - July 30
  • Verified voting through secure platform
  • One vote per verified individual
  • Real-time monitoring for integrity

Transparency: Final scores and voting breakdown published post-ceremony.

Final Stages & Ceremony

What happens during the business interviews (October 1-30)?

Comprehensive Interview Process:

Format:

  • Panel interview (3-5 judges)
  • 45-60 minute sessions
  • CEO/Founder + 2 senior team members required
  • Virtual or in-person options
  • Recorded for verification purposes

Focus Areas:

  • Business strategy and vision
  • Crisis management capabilities
  • Innovation roadmap
  • Leadership philosophy
  • Sustainability commitments
  • Growth plans for Liberia

Preparation: Shortlisted businesses receive interview guidelines 2 weeks in advance.
