How to Register Your Business

To get business dashboard access, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the main website: liberiabusinessawardslr.com
  2. Click "Register Your Business" button
  3. Fill out the complete registration form
  4. Submit required business documents
  5. Wait for admin verification (1-2 business days)
  6. Receive login credentials via email

Need help? Email: admin@liberiabusinessawardslr.com
