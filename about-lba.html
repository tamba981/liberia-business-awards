About Liberia Business Awards

Recognizing Local Excellence, Celebrating National Impact

Our Story

The Liberia Business Awards was founded in 2022 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a platform that celebrates and recognizes the incredible entrepreneurial spirit driving Liberia's economic growth.

What began as a small initiative to honor local businesses has grown into Liberia's most prestigious business recognition program. Since our founding, we've recognized over 500 businesses, connected thousands of entrepreneurs, and created a vibrant ecosystem of innovation and collaboration.

Our journey reflects the resilience and determination of Liberian businesses. From startups in Monrovia to agricultural enterprises in rural counties, we've witnessed firsthand the transformative power of recognition and community support.

Our Mission

To identify, celebrate, and support outstanding Liberian businesses that demonstrate excellence, innovation, and positive impact in their communities and industries.

Our Vision

To build a network of 10,000 entrepreneurs nationwide, united by innovation, collaboration, and excellence, driving sustainable economic development across Liberia.
