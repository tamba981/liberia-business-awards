Who We Are

The Liberia Business Awards is the nation's premier recognition platform for businesses across all 18 major economic sectors. We provide comprehensive coverage from Agriculture to Sports, ensuring every business in Liberia has the opportunity to be recognized for excellence.

Our 18-sector framework encompasses the complete Liberian economy, with 72 specialized award categories designed to celebrate achievements at every level - from startups to established enterprises, fostering economic growth and inspiring the next generation of entrepreneurs.

J-Palm Liberia - Success Story

Kernel Fresh - Sustainable Agriculture Award

Founded: 2011

Founder: Mahmud Johnson

Location: Monrovia, Liberia with operations across rural communities

Employees: 200+ direct employment, 500+ smallholder farmers

Specialization: Sustainable palm oil production & premium consumer goods

Achievement: Africa's Top 10 Business Heroes (2019)

The Kernel Fresh Journey

J-Palm Liberia (JPL) was founded with a visionary mission: to produce premium consumer goods while empowering local communities. Through their innovative "Kernel Fresh" brand, they have transformed how sustainable palm oil production creates value across Liberia's agricultural sector.

National Impact & Achievements

  • Community Empowerment: Fostered hundreds of jobs and empowered smallholder farmers through ethical supply chains
  • Premium Products: Developed beauty essentials and clean energy products under the "Kernel Fresh" brand
  • Sustainable Model: Pioneered ethical palm oil production that protects biodiversity and supports local ecosystems
  • National Footprint: Built a strong distribution network across Liberia, promoting Liberian-made products
  • International Recognition: Honored by African Development Bank Group for sustainable enterprise development

Philosophy for Progress

"When we talk about productivity in the digital age, it's really about people. Productivity is not just about output per worker — it's about opportunities per person."

— J-Palm Liberia Leadership

Post-Award Recognition

  • Featured as one of Africa's Top 10 Business Heroes (2019)
  • Recognized by the African Development Bank Group for sustainable development impact
  • Expanded product lines to international markets
  • Increased smallholder farmer partnerships by 150%
  • Launched community training programs in sustainable agriculture

Future Vision

As Liberia continues to rise, J-Palm Liberia stands as a shining example of how innovation, sustainability, and social impact can coexist — driving real change from the ground up. Their vision is to become West Africa's leading sustainable consumer goods company by 2030.

Education & Professional Development Award

Founded: 2021

Founder: Mohammed Kerkulah

Partners: Growth Accelerator Liberia with UNDP Support

Location: S.D. Cooper Road, Paynesville

The Professional Development Revolution

In a country where access to quality professional training has often been limited, Gonet Academy is changing the narrative. Founded by Mohammed Kerkulah and scaled by Growth Accelerator Liberia with UNDP support, the Academy has become a national model for career development, mentorship, and innovation.

Structured Cohort-Based Learning

  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Equips professionals with practical knowledge, digital skills, and leadership tools
  • Mentorship Program: Connects students with industry leaders and successful entrepreneurs
  • Real-World Projects: Hands-on learning through business simulations and community projects
  • Digital Skills Focus: Training in essential digital tools, online marketing, and e-commerce
  • Networking Opportunities: Access to alumni network and industry partnerships

Impact & Growth Journey

  • Cohort 1 to 12: Successfully trained and certified hundreds of emerging leaders
  • 500+ Graduates: Professionals empowered across multiple disciplines
  • Fostered Entrepreneurship: 40+ businesses launched by alumni
  • Employability Enhancement: 85% of graduates report career advancement within 6 months
  • Community Impact: Alumni projects benefiting local communities across Liberia

Cohort 13 & Future Vision

As Cohort 13 prepares to begin, Gonet Academy's commitment to continuous growth and inclusive education remains stronger than ever. The Academy continues to evolve its curriculum based on market demands and technological advancements.

Testimonials

"Gonet Academy transformed my career. The practical skills and network I gained helped me launch my digital marketing agency that now serves clients across Liberia."

— Sarah K., Cohort 8 Graduate

National Development Impact

Gonet Academy's story reflects Liberia's potential — proof that when vision meets consistency, education becomes the foundation for national progress. Each cohort represents a new wave of transformation — empowering students to add value to their careers and contribute meaningfully to national development.

Green Farms Liberia - Success Story

Agriculture Business of the Year 2024

Founded: 2018

Location: Bong County, Liberia

Employees: 150+

Specialization: Organic Agriculture & Sustainable Farming

The Journey

Green Farms Liberia started as a small family-owned farm in 2018 with just 5 acres of land. Through innovative farming techniques and sustainable practices, they have expanded to over 500 acres across three counties, becoming one of Liberia's leading agricultural producers.

Impact & Achievements

  • Increased crop yield by 300% through sustainable farming methods
  • Created employment opportunities for 150+ local community members
  • Partnered with international markets for export of organic produce
  • Implemented farmer training programs benefiting 500+ local farmers
  • Reduced environmental impact through organic farming practices

Post-Award Growth

Since winning the Agriculture Business of the Year award in 2024, Green Farms Liberia has experienced remarkable growth:

  • 40% increase in partnership inquiries
  • Secured $2M in additional funding for expansion
  • Expanded export operations to 3 new countries
  • Launched "Green Future" initiative to train young farmers

Future Vision

Green Farms Liberia aims to become West Africa's leading organic agriculture hub by 2030, with plans to expand to 1,000 acres and establish a state-of-the-art agricultural research center.

Liberian Gold Coffee - Success Story

Export Excellence Award 2024

Founded: 2015

Location: Nimba County, Liberia

Employees: 200+

Specialization: Premium Coffee Export

From Local to Global

Liberian Gold Coffee began with a vision to showcase Liberia's premium coffee beans to the world. Starting with small-scale farming in Nimba County, they have transformed into Liberia's leading coffee exporter, bringing Liberian coffee to international markets.

Export Milestones

  • First Liberian coffee company to export to European markets
  • Established supply chains to 15+ countries worldwide
  • Achieved international quality certifications (Fair Trade, Organic)
  • Developed unique coffee blends for different international markets
  • Implemented sustainable packaging solutions

Community Impact

Liberian Gold Coffee works directly with 500+ local coffee farmers, providing training, fair prices, and sustainable farming practices that have increased farmer incomes by 150%.

Post-Award Expansion

  • Expanded export operations to 8 new countries
  • Increased production capacity by 300%
  • Secured $3M in export financing
  • Launched 3 new premium coffee product lines
  • Estigned partnerships with international coffee chains

Future Vision

Liberian Gold Coffee aims to make Liberian coffee a globally recognized premium brand by 2030, with plans to establish coffee tourism experiences and launch coffee processing training centers across Liberia.

OUR FOCUS & REACH

The Liberia Business Awards is a nationally rooted and globally connected platform dedicated to elevating Liberian businesses.

Our primary focus is Liberia, where we recognize, support, and showcase excellence across all counties and economic sectors. From startups to established enterprises, LBA exists to strengthen the backbone of Liberia's private sector.

Beyond national borders, LBA maintains strategic interest and engagement across Africa and key global markets. These regions represent partnerships, diaspora connections, development institutions, investors, and platforms that support trade, innovation, and business growth for Liberian enterprises.

Through this national-to-global approach, LBA serves as a bridge between Liberian businesses and international opportunity, positioning local enterprises for visibility, credibility, and sustainable expansion.

🇱🇷 Liberia first. Africa connected. Global impact. 🌍

Our Vision

"To become Liberia's leading national platform for business recognition, visibility, and private sector excellence, showcasing the entrepreneurs and enterprises driving innovation, job creation, and economic transformation."

We position Liberia's most impactful businesses on a credible national and international stage, strengthening the country's reputation as a growing hub for entrepreneurship and investment.

Our Mission

To recognize, celebrate, and elevate businesses and entrepreneurs that are contributing to Liberia's economic growth and social development.

Providing a credible national recognition platform
Increasing visibility and credibility for entrepreneurs
Connecting businesses with partners and investors
Promoting innovation and ethical business practices
Supporting inclusive private sector growth
