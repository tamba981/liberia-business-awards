4 Judging Process

4.1 Judging Panel

The judging panel consists of independent industry experts, business leaders, and professionals selected for their expertise and impartiality. Judges are required to:

  • Sign confidentiality agreements
  • Declare any conflicts of interest
  • Evaluate submissions based on established criteria
  • Maintain impartiality throughout the process

4.2 Evaluation Criteria

Criteria Weight Description
Business Impact 30% Economic contribution, job creation, community development
Innovation 25% Creativity, unique solutions, market differentiation
Growth & Sustainability 20% Financial performance, scalability, long-term viability
Leadership & Governance 15% Management quality, ethical practices, corporate governance
Public Voting 10% Public votes (each vote = 1 point, jury votes = 3 points)

4.3 Final Decision

The judging panel's decision is final and binding. No correspondence will be entered into regarding the judging process or results. Winners will be announced at the awards ceremony on December 19, 2026.

5 Voting Rules

5.1 Voting Period

Public voting runs from June 1 to July 30 annually. All votes must be cast within this period to be counted.

5.2 Voting Guidelines

  • Each person may vote once per business using a valid email address
  • Email verification is required before votes are counted
  • Votes are rated on a scale of 1-10
  • Public votes count as 1 point each
  • Jury votes count as 3 points each
  • Votes cannot be changed after submission

5.3 Voting Integrity

We monitor voting patterns for irregularities. Any detected fraudulent voting (including bot voting, vote buying, or multiple votes from the same email) will result in disqualification of affected votes and potential disqualification of the business from the awards.

5.4 Vote Weight Calculation

Final scores are calculated as: (Average Public Vote × 0.6) + (Average Jury Vote × 0.4)

Example: If a business receives average public vote of 8.5 and average jury vote of 9.0, final score = (8.5 × 0.6) + (9.0 × 0.4) = 5.1 + 3.6 = 8.7
