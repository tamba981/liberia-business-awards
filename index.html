Who We Are

The Liberia Business Awards is the nation's premier recognition platform for businesses across all 18 major economic sectors. We provide comprehensive coverage from Agriculture to Sports, ensuring every business in Liberia has the opportunity to be recognized for excellence.

Our 18-sector framework encompasses the complete Liberian economy, with 72 specialized award categories designed to celebrate achievements at every level - from startups to established enterprises, fostering economic growth and inspiring the next generation of entrepreneurs.

  • National Footprint: Built a strong distribution network across Liberia, promoting Liberian-made products

Green Farms Liberia - Success Story

Agriculture Business of the Year 2024

Founded: 2018

Location: Bong County, Liberia

Employees: 150+

Specialization: Organic Agriculture & Sustainable Farming

The Journey

Green Farms Liberia started as a small family-owned farm in 2018 with just 5 acres of land. Through innovative farming techniques and sustainable practices, they have expanded to over 500 acres across three counties, becoming one of Liberia's leading agricultural producers.

Impact & Achievements

  • Increased crop yield by 300% through sustainable farming methods
  • Created employment opportunities for 150+ local community members
  • Partnered with international markets for export of organic produce
  • Implemented farmer training programs benefiting 500+ local farmers
  • Reduced environmental impact through organic farming practices

Post-Award Growth

Since winning the Agriculture Business of the Year award in 2024, Green Farms Liberia has experienced remarkable growth:

  • 40% increase in partnership inquiries
  • Secured $2M in additional funding for expansion
  • Expanded export operations to 3 new countries
  • Launched "Green Future" initiative to train young farmers

Future Vision

Green Farms Liberia aims to become West Africa's leading organic agriculture hub by 2030, with plans to expand to 1,000 acres and establish a state-of-the-art agricultural research center.

Technology Innovation Award 2024

Founded: 2020

Location: Monrovia, Liberia

Innovation Journey

TechHub Liberia emerged as a response to the growing need for digital solutions and tech talent development in Liberia. Starting with just 3 developers, they have grown into a leading technology innovation hub driving digital transformation across various sectors.

Key Innovations

  • Developed Liberia's first comprehensive e-government platform
  • Created mobile payment solutions for rural communities
  • Built custom ERP systems for local businesses
  • Developed educational technology platforms for remote learning
  • Pioneered AI-powered agricultural advisory services

Training Impact

Through their Tech Academy, TechHub Liberia has trained over 500 young Liberians in software development, data science, and digital skills, with 85% of graduates securing employment in the tech sector.

Post-Award Achievements

  • Secured partnerships with 3 international tech companies
  • Expanded operations to 2 new counties
  • Launched Liberia's first tech incubator program
  • Increased client base by 200%
  • Received $1.5M in venture funding

Future Roadmap

TechHub Liberia aims to establish West Africa's most advanced technology innovation center by 2027, focusing on artificial intelligence, blockchain, and renewable energy technologies.

Liberian Gold Coffee - Success Story

Export Excellence Award 2024

Founded: 2015

Location: Nimba County, Liberia

Employees: 200+

Specialization: Premium Coffee Export

From Local to Global

Liberian Gold Coffee began with a vision to showcase Liberia's premium coffee beans to the world. Starting with small-scale farming in Nimba County, they have transformed into Liberia's leading coffee exporter, bringing Liberian coffee to international markets.

Export Milestones

  • First Liberian coffee company to export to European markets
  • Established supply chains to 15+ countries worldwide
  • Achieved international quality certifications (Fair Trade, Organic)
  • Developed unique coffee blends for different international markets
  • Implemented sustainable packaging solutions

Community Impact

Liberian Gold Coffee works directly with 500+ local coffee farmers, providing training, fair prices, and sustainable farming practices that have increased farmer incomes by 150%.

Post-Award Expansion

  • Expanded export operations to 8 new countries
  • Increased production capacity by 300%
  • Secured $3M in export financing
  • Launched 3 new premium coffee product lines
  • Estigned partnerships with international coffee chains

Future Vision

Liberian Gold Coffee aims to make Liberian coffee a globally recognized premium brand by 2030, with plans to establish coffee tourism experiences and launch coffee processing training centers across Liberia.
