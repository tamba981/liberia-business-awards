2 Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for nomination and participation in Liberia Business Awards 3.0, businesses and individuals must meet the following criteria:

Category Requirements
Businesses
  • Registered and operating in Liberia
  • Minimum 1 year of continuous operation
  • Valid business registration documents
  • Compliance with Liberian business laws
Entrepreneurs
  • Liberian citizenship or legal residency
  • Active business ownership/leadership
  • Demonstrable business achievements
  • No criminal convictions related to business
Startups
  • Business less than 5 years old
  • Innovative product/service
  • Growth potential demonstrated
  • Minimum 3 months of operation

Disqualification Grounds

Businesses may be disqualified for: false information, unethical practices, legal violations, bankruptcy proceedings, or previous award revocation.

3 Nomination Process

3.1 Submission Guidelines

  • All nominations must be submitted through the official LBA website or authorized channels
  • Nominations open on March 1, yearly and close on May 30 (5:59 PM) Liberia Time
  • Each business may be nominated in up to 3 categories
  • Self-nominations are accepted and encouraged
  • All information provided must be accurate and verifiable

3.2 Required Documentation

Nominations must include:

Business registration documents
Tax clearance certificate
Business profile/description
Achievements and impact evidence

3.3 Entry Fees

There are no entry fees for nominations. The Liberia Business Awards is committed to making business recognition accessible to all qualified Liberian businesses regardless of size or financial capacity.

4 Judging Process

4.1 Judging Panel

The judging panel consists of independent industry experts, business leaders, and professionals selected for their expertise and impartiality. Judges are required to:

  • Sign confidentiality agreements
  • Declare any conflicts of interest
  • Evaluate submissions based on established criteria
  • Maintain impartiality throughout the process

4.2 Evaluation Criteria

Criteria Weight Description
Business Impact 30% Economic contribution, job creation, community development
Innovation 25% Creativity, unique solutions, market differentiation
Growth & Sustainability 20% Financial performance, scalability, long-term viability
Leadership & Governance 15% Management quality, ethical practices, corporate governance
Customer/Market Feedback 10% Customer satisfaction, market reputation, testimonials

4.3 Final Decision

The judging panel's decision is final and binding. No correspondence will be entered into regarding the judging process or results. Winners will be announced at the awards ceremony on May 30, 2026.
