Judging Criteria Insights

What Judges Look For

  • Innovation (25%): How creatively does the business solve problems or serve customers?
  • Growth & Sustainability (25%): Demonstrated growth with a sustainable business model
  • Impact (25%): Positive effect on employees, community, and Liberia's economy
  • Leadership (15%): Quality of management and vision
  • Presentation (10%): Clarity and compelling nature of the nomination

Pro Tips from the Judges

Show, Don't Just Tell

Use specific numbers and examples instead of generic statements about success.

Highlight Your Team

Judges value businesses that invest in and develop their employees.

Demonstrate Sustainability

Explain how your business will continue to grow and impact Liberia long-term.
