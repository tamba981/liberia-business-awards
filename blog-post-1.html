1 Tell a Compelling Story

Judges read hundreds of nominations. What makes yours memorable? It's not just what you've achieved, but how you achieved it and why it matters to Liberia.

💡 Pro Tip: Use the "Hero's Journey" Framework

Structure your nomination like a story:

  • The Challenge: What problem did you identify in Liberia's market?
  • The Journey: How did you overcome obstacles?
  • The Transformation: What did you learn and how did you grow?
  • The Impact: How did your solution benefit Liberia?

Example: Liberian Women in Agriculture

❌ Weak: "We trained 50 women farmers in modern techniques."

✅ Strong: "In rural Bong County, where women farmers struggled with 40% crop loss, we introduced sustainable farming techniques that transformed not just harvests, but entire communities. Meet Sarah, a mother of four, who now leads a cooperative of 30 women exporting to regional markets."

2 Provide Concrete Evidence

Numbers speak louder than words. But not just any numbers—meaningful metrics that demonstrate real impact.

DO Include

  • ✓ Year-over-year growth percentages
  • ✓ Jobs created (direct and indirect)
  • ✓ Revenue tied to specific initiatives
  • ✓ Customer testimonials with results
  • ✓ Before-and-after comparisons

DON'T Include

  • ✗ Vague statements ("we grew a lot")
  • ✗ Unverifiable claims
  • ✗ Industry jargon without explanation
  • ✗ Overly technical details
  • ✗ Generic platitudes

4 Demonstrate Innovation & Adaptability

Liberia's business landscape is evolving. Show how you're leading the change.

Innovation in Action:

💻 Tech Integration: "When traditional supply chains failed during the rainy season, we developed a mobile app connecting farmers directly to markets, reducing spoilage by 65%."

🔧 Local Solutions: "Instead of importing expensive machinery, we engineered affordable, solar-powered equipment using 70% locally sourced materials."

5 Professional Presentation Matters

First impressions count. A well-organized, error-free nomination shows professionalism.

📋 Final Checklist Before Submission:

  • Proofread for spelling and grammar errors
  • Format consistently (headings, bullet points, spacing)
  • Attach supporting documents in required format
  • Include high-quality photos (minimum 5)
  • Add contact information for verification
  • Submit before the deadline (early submissions preferred)
